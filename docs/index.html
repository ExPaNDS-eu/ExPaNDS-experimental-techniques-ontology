Scope of PaNET

PaNET is a comprehensive, semi-hierarchical vocabulary dedicated to Photon and Neutron Experimental Techniques. A technique is defined by four major aspects

  • experimental physical probe
  • experimental probe
  • functional dependence
  • purpose

Something is a technique in the scope of PaNET, if

  • the technique is in use, or has been used in the past, at a large European photon or neutron facility. Facility-members of LEAPS and LENS are examples of such facilities, as are ESFRI-recognised facilities such as ELI.
  • the technique takes advantage of one of the facility's beamlines. A technique is not eligible merely for taking place within a facility's campus.
  • the technique distinguishes itself from other techniques NOT only by the choice of the sample or the sample's state. This criterion may be overruled if there is a very strong view from the user community that the sample is fundamental to the technique definition.
  • the technique distinguishes itself from another technique by an essential dependency on some well-defined sample interaction. A laser pump-probe is an example of such an interaction.

Subscribe to our Mailing Lists

There are two mailing lists for different purposes available. The Announcement List will provide detailed information on releases and will only be sent once or twice a year. It is dedicated to service providers that use PaNET. The Maintenance List is used to invite to the weekly meetings and announce the agendas. It can also be used to initiate discussions and highlight open issues.

  • Go to the public page of the mailing list (Announcement, Maintenance).
  • Enter your email address and name.
  • Press the blue button "I subscribe to list panet-announce".
  • The page updates and you need to confirm (blue button).
  • You receive a mail by SYMPA with the title "DESY Mailing lists services" and including a link. Click it.
  • A German text asks you to confirm your subscription by clicking the button "Bestätigen" while the "Abonnieren" is marked with a checkmark.
  • You receive a welcome mail by panet-announce-request@desy.de or panet-maintenance-request@desy.de
